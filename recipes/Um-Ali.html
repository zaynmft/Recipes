Easy Om Ali Recipe

This is a very famous dessrt from Egypt. The flavor and texture all come together to make wonderful creamy dessert

Ingredients

  • 1 (17.5 ounce) package frozen puff pastry sheets, thawed
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 cup chopped hazelnuts
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 cup flaked coconut
  • 1¼ cups white sugar, divided
  • 4 cups milk
  • ½ cup heavy cream
